# PowerBi_Customer_Sales_Dashboard
Power BI dashboard for sales and customer analytics with KPI tracking, trend analysis, and top customer insights.

## Tools
- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX

## Features
- KPI cards (Sales, Profit, Orders)
- Sales by sub-category & region
- Top 10 customers
- Sales trend over time
- Interactive filters

## Insights
- Revenue concentrated among top customers  
- Sales uneven across sub-categories  
- Profit varies despite high sales  
- Sales trends over time
