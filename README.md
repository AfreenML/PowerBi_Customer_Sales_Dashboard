# PowerBi_Customer_Sales_Dashboard
  Interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing sales, customer behavior, and profitability using Power Query and DAX.
  
## Tools
- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX

## Features
- KPI cards (Sales, Profit, Orders)
- Sales by sub-category & region
- Top 10 customers
- Sales trend over time
- Interactive filters

- ## 💡 Key Insights
- A small segment of high-value customers contributes a significant share of total revenue, indicating customer concentration risk and the need for targeted retention strategies.
- Sales are heavily skewed toward specific sub-categories, identifying key revenue drivers while highlighting underperforming segments that may require optimization or repositioning.
- Regional profitability varies, suggesting opportunities to replicate high-performing strategies in weaker regions and improve overall business performance.
- Time-series analysis shows consistent sales patterns with fluctuations, indicating potential seasonality and the need for better demand forecasting and inventory planning.
- A mismatch between sales and profit in certain areas suggests margin inefficiencies, highlighting the importance of pricing strategy and cost control.
- The analysis highlights opportunities to improve profitability by focusing on high-margin products and optimizing low-performing segments.
